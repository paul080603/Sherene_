13 May

Main toh tera tabhi ho chuka tha, jab pehli baar teri khamoshi ne mujhse baat ki. Woh noor jo tere andar se aata hai, usne meri andheri raton ko roshni se bhar diya.
Main toh tera deewana tabhi ban chuka tha, jab tere hone se kamra mehek uthta tha. Na shor tha, na dhamaka, bas tu thi, aur sab kuch theek lagta tha.
Teri aankhon mein kuch aisa hai Sherene, jo toofan ko bhi dheere se roka karta hai. Nazuk si hai tu phool jaisi, par teri ruh mein ek aag bhi jalta hai.
Tujhse mil ke pehli baar jaana, ki ghar sirf chaar deewaron mein nahi hota. Ghar woh hota hai jahan dil ko chain mile, aur mera dil, tere paas aake, ruk jaata hai.
Aaj tareeq hai terah May, aur main bhi toh hoon tera. Is janam ke aant tak, rooh se tera, bas ek sawaal hai, dheere se puchta hoon...
Thu kab banegi meri, Sherene?
